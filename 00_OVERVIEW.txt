SOVEREIGN AI STARTER PACK — OLLAMA EDITION
00 — Overview

------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT IS THIS PACK?
------------------------------------------------------------

This is a beginner-friendly guide to running AI on your own computer.

You will learn how to:
  • Install Ollama (the easiest way to run local AI)
  • Download and run open-source language models
  • Add an optional browser chat interface (Open WebUI)
  • Choose the right model for your hardware
  • Stay private — no cloud, no accounts, no data leaks

You do not need coding experience. You do not need a powerful gaming PC.
You do need patience for the first download — models are large files.


------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT IS LOCAL AI?
------------------------------------------------------------

Local AI means the AI runs on YOUR machine — your laptop, desktop, or
mini PC — instead of on a company's server in the cloud.

When you use cloud AI (like a website chatbot), your messages travel
over the internet to someone else's computer. They can log it, store it,
or use it to train future models.

When you use local AI:
  • Your messages stay on your device
  • No account or login is required (with Ollama)
  • You control what models are installed
  • You can delete everything anytime
  • It works offline after models are downloaded


------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT IS SOVEREIGN COMPUTE?
------------------------------------------------------------

"Sovereign compute" means YOU own the compute. Your hardware. Your data.
Your rules.

In practice:
  • AI runs locally, not in the cloud
  • No third party reads your prompts or outputs
  • No subscription required to keep using what you installed
  • You decide what stays on disk and what gets deleted

This pack is built around sovereign compute from day one.


------------------------------------------------------------
WHY OLLAMA FIRST?
------------------------------------------------------------

There are many local AI tools. This pack starts with Ollama because:

  • Free and open-source
  • Works on Mac, Windows, and Linux
  • One-line install on most systems
  • Simple commands: ollama run <model-name>
  • Huge library of models (Llama, Qwen, Mistral, Gemma, Phi, and more)
  • No account required
  • Active development and frequent updates

Once you understand Ollama, switching to other tools (GPT4All, LM Studio,
Jan, Kobold) is much easier. See 05_OTHER_POPULAR_TOOLS.txt.


------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT LOCAL AI CAN DO (AND CANNOT DO)
------------------------------------------------------------

CAN DO:
  • Answer questions and explain topics
  • Help you write, edit, and brainstorm text
  • Summarize documents you paste in
  • Help with coding questions
  • Run completely offline after setup

CANNOT DO (OR DOES POORLY ON SMALL HARDWARE):
  • Match the speed of cloud AI on a weak laptop
  • Run the largest models without enough RAM
  • Guarantee 100% factual accuracy (always verify important info)
  • Replace professional advice (legal, medical, financial)

Local AI is powerful and private. It is not magic. Model quality and your
hardware both matter.

------------------------------------------------------------
YOUR FIRST 15 MINUTES
------------------------------------------------------------

  1. Install Ollama (01_INSTALL_OLLAMA.txt)
  2. Open Terminal (Mac/Linux) or PowerShell (Windows)
  3. Type:  ollama run llama3.2
  4. Wait for the download (first time only — can take several minutes)
  5. Type a message and press Enter
  6. You are now running local AI

That is the whole point of this pack. Everything else is depth.


------------------------------------------------------------
A NOTE ON PRIVACY
------------------------------------------------------------

Local AI is private by default — but only if you keep it local.

  • Do not paste passwords, bank details, or medical records into any AI
  • If you add cloud features later (some tools offer them), read the
    settings carefully and turn them off if you want to stay local
  • Delete models and chat history when you are done if you share the PC

Full privacy guide: 09_SAFETY_AND_PRIVACY.txt
