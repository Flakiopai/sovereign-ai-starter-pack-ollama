SOVEREIGN AI STARTER PACK — OLLAMA EDITION
09 — Safety and Privacy

------------------------------------------------------------
CORE PRINCIPLE
------------------------------------------------------------

Sovereign compute means your AI runs on your hardware, under your
control. No cloud by default. No third party reading your prompts.
Your machine. Your data. Your rules.


------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT "LOCAL" ACTUALLY MEANS
------------------------------------------------------------

When you use Ollama correctly:
  • Chat inference happens on your CPU/GPU
  • Model files sit on your disk
  • No account is required to run models
  • You can delete models and history anytime

"Caveats" beginners should know:
  • Downloading a model uses the internet (one-time per model)
  • Some companion apps offer optional cloud sync — turn it OFF if you
    want strict local-only
  • Physical access to your PC = access to your files (lock your screen)


------------------------------------------------------------
KEEPING YOUR SETUP SOVEREIGN
------------------------------------------------------------

  [ ] Ollama installed from official source (ollama.com)
  [ ] Models pulled only when you choose
  [ ] Open WebUI (if used) runs on localhost — not exposed to internet
  [ ] Cloud / telemetry toggles OFF in any secondary app (GPT4All, etc.)
  [ ] No personal secrets pasted into chat (passwords, SSN, bank data)
  [ ] Shared PC: log out, delete sensitive chats, or use separate OS user
  [ ] Models removed when no longer needed (ollama rm)


------------------------------------------------------------
DATA ON YOUR DISK
------------------------------------------------------------

Things stored locally:
  • Downloaded models (~/.ollama/models/)
  • Open WebUI chat history (Docker volume or app data folder)
  • Logs from various apps (usually small)

To wipe Ollama models:
  ollama list
  ollama rm <each-model-name>

To remove everything Ollama-related, delete the .ollama folder in your
user directory (only if you intend a full reset).


------------------------------------------------------------
NETWORK EXPOSURE — DO NOT SKIP THIS
------------------------------------------------------------

By default, Open WebUI on localhost:3000 is only reachable from YOUR
computer. That is what you want.

Do NOT:
  • Port-forward Open WebUI to the public internet without hardening
  • Share your local AI URL on social media
  • Run unauthenticated Open WebUI on a network you do not trust

Advanced users who need remote access should use VPN solutions (e.g.
Tailscale) — outside beginner scope of this pack.


------------------------------------------------------------
RESPONSIBLE USE
------------------------------------------------------------

Local AI does not remove ethical responsibility.

  • Do not use AI to generate harassment, threats, or illegal content
  • Do not use AI to impersonate real people without consent
  • Verify important facts — models can be wrong
  • Do not rely on AI for medical, legal, or financial decisions
  • Respect copyright and others' intellectual property
  • Follow your local laws and workplace policies


------------------------------------------------------------
PRIVACY WITH SENSITIVE TOPICS
------------------------------------------------------------

Even on local AI:
  • Treat chats like files on your desktop — recoverable until deleted
  • Avoid pasting medical records, legal documents, or HR complaints
    unless you accept the risk of local storage
  • Encrypt your disk (FileVault on Mac, BitLocker on Windows) if the
    machine could be lost or stolen


------------------------------------------------------------
KIDS AND FAMILY COMPUTERS
------------------------------------------------------------

  • Local AI has no built-in parental filter by default
  • Model outputs can be inappropriate if prompted that way
  • Supervise young users or restrict access at the OS level
  • Consider a dedicated user account for AI experiments


------------------------------------------------------------
THIRD-PARTY TOOLS
------------------------------------------------------------

If you try GPT4All, LM Studio, Jan, or others (05_OTHER_POPULAR_TOOLS.txt):

  • Read each app's privacy settings on first launch
  • Disable analytics, cloud sync, and "improve our service" options
     if you want sovereignty
  • Assume defaults may change between versions — re-check after updates


------------------------------------------------------------
HARDWARE SAFETY
------------------------------------------------------------

  • AI workloads heat CPU/GPU — ensure vents are clear
  • Laptops: use on hard surfaces during long sessions
  • Stop if the machine is uncomfortably hot
  • Unplug and cool down if thermal shutdown occurs


------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT THIS PACK WILL NEVER ASK YOU TO DO
------------------------------------------------------------

  • Upload your chats to a cloud service for "better answers"
  • Create accounts with third-party AI providers to use Ollama
  • Disable your firewall to install local AI
  • Share personal data in prompts for "setup" or "activation"
  • Pay a subscription to keep using what you already installed locally


------------------------------------------------------------
SOVEREIGN MINDSET
------------------------------------------------------------

The goal is independence:
  • You can work offline after models are downloaded
  • You choose what stays and what gets deleted
  • You are not locked to one vendor's cloud
  • You can switch tools without losing the underlying idea

Local AI is a skill and a stance — not just an app install.
