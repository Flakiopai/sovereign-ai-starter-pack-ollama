SOVEREIGN AI STARTER PACK — OLLAMA EDITION
08 — FAQ

------------------------------------------------------------
GENERAL
------------------------------------------------------------

Q: Do I need to pay for anything?
A: Ollama and the models in this guide are free. Open WebUI is free.
   Optional tools (GPT4All, LM Studio, Jan) are free for local use.
   You pay with electricity and your own hardware — no subscription.

Q: Do I need an internet connection?
A: Only for installing software and downloading models. After that,
   chat works fully offline.

Q: Do I need a ChatGPT or OpenAI account?
A: No. Ollama does not require any cloud account.

Q: Is this the same as ChatGPT?
A: Similar idea (you chat with AI), different technology. Local models
   run on your PC. Quality and speed depend on your model and hardware.
   Cloud ChatGPT runs on OpenAI's servers and needs an account.

Q: Will my data be used to train AI?
A: With a proper local setup, your chats stay on your machine. Ollama
   does not send prompts to the cloud by default. Some optional tools
   may offer cloud features — turn them off in settings if you want
   full privacy. See 09_SAFETY_AND_PRIVACY.txt.


------------------------------------------------------------
INSTALL & SETUP
------------------------------------------------------------

Q: Which operating system is best?
A: All three work. Apple Silicon Macs are excellent for beginners.
   Windows with NVIDIA GPU is great for speed. Linux is popular with
   power users. Use what you already have.

Q: Do I need to know programming?
A: No. You need to copy commands into Terminal (Mac/Linux) or
   PowerShell (Windows). This pack walks you through each step.

Q: What is Terminal / PowerShell?
A: A text window where you type commands. Mac: Terminal app.
   Windows: PowerShell or Command Prompt. It looks plain but is safe
   when you follow trusted instructions.

Q: Is Open WebUI required?
A: No. It is optional. Ollama works from the terminal alone.

Q: Do I need Docker?
A: Only if you use the recommended Open WebUI install method.
   Ollama itself does not need Docker.


------------------------------------------------------------
MODELS
------------------------------------------------------------

Q: Which model should I download first?
A: llama3.2 for most people. phi3 or mistral if you have 8 GB RAM
   and want speed. See 04_MODEL_RECOMMENDATIONS.txt.

Q: How big are model downloads?
A: Roughly 2 GB for tiny models, 4–5 GB for 7B–8B models, 40 GB+
   for the largest. Check free disk space before pulling.

Q: Can I delete a model?
A: Yes.  ollama rm <model-name>  frees disk space. You can re-download
   anytime with ollama pull.

Q: How many models should I keep?
A: Start with one. Add more only when you know you need them. They
   eat disk space and tempt you to run too many at once.

Q: Why is the first response so slow?
A: The model loads into RAM on first use. Later messages in the same
   session are faster. CPU-only machines are always slower than GPU.

Q: Are local models as smart as GPT-4?
A: The largest cloud models still lead on some hard tasks. But modern
   7B–8B local models are surprisingly good for everyday chat, writing,
   and learning — especially with privacy benefits.


------------------------------------------------------------
HARDWARE
------------------------------------------------------------

Q: Can I run this on a laptop?
A: Yes. Use smaller models on 8 GB RAM laptops. 16 GB is comfortable.

Q: Do I need an NVIDIA GPU?
A: No. GPU helps speed. CPU works for small models.

Q: My Mac has 8 GB RAM. Can I still do this?
A: Yes. Use phi3, llama3.2:1b, or qwen2.5:0.5b. Close other apps.

Q: Can I use an old PC?
A: If it has 8 GB RAM and a modern 64-bit OS, try phi3. Very old
   machines (4 GB RAM) will struggle.


------------------------------------------------------------
PRIVACY & SECURITY
------------------------------------------------------------

Q: Is local AI 100% private?
A: Private from cloud companies — yes, when configured correctly. Anyone
   with physical access to your computer can still read files on disk.
   Shared family PCs need extra care.

Q: Can I use this at work?
A: Check your employer's IT policy. Local AI may be fine or forbidden.
   Do not paste confidential work data into any AI without permission.

Q: Should I paste passwords or bank info into local AI?
A: No. Never paste secrets into any AI, local or cloud.

Q: Does Ollama phone home?
A: Ollama downloads models from the internet when you request them.
   Normal chat inference runs locally. Check Ollama's docs for updates
   on telemetry in your version.

------------------------------------------------------------
OTHER TOOLS
------------------------------------------------------------

Q: Should I switch from Ollama to GPT4All or LM Studio?
A: Only if you want a different interface. Ollama is the best starting
   point. See 05_OTHER_POPULAR_TOOLS.txt for comparisons.

Q: Can I run Ollama and LM Studio together?
A: You can install both, but do not run large models in both at the
   same time — they compete for RAM.


------------------------------------------------------------
UPDATES
------------------------------------------------------------

Q: How do I update Ollama?
A: Download the latest installer from ollama.com or re-run the Linux
   install script. Models usually stay on disk after update.

Q: How do I know my pack version?
A: See VERSION.TXT and changelog.txt in this folder.
